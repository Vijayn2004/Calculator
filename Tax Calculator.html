The ₹12 Lakh Zero Tax Magic

In the New Regime (2025-26), if your taxable income is up to ₹12 Lakh, you get a full rebate (Sec 87A). Combine this with the ₹75,000 standard deduction, and a salary of ₹12.75 Lakh becomes effectively tax-free!

Massive Slab Upgrade

In year 2000, 30% tax started at just ₹1.5 Lakh. Today, in the new regime, the 30% tax slab only hits you when your income crosses ₹24 Lakh!
